Describing People!

This is probably the best part of getting someone attached to a character. A good description of a character is important, it gives readers a person in their head to 'see'. They'll let they're imagination fill in the blanks, however they'll only see what you show them first.

It's important to note that you can't info-dump the character descriptions at the start. If you have an entire page dedicated to how someone 'looks' and 'acts', chances are people aren't going to care too much. You can reveal more of them gradually throughout the writing, especially their personality and habits(see habit writing). It's the same with color, we don't need the one by one description of 'his eyes are blue, his hair is black, he's wearing a black dress shirt and black pants'. No, gradually write it in.
