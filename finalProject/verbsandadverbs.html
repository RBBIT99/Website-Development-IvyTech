Verbs!

When you're describing an action, any action, chances are you're using a verb! Verbs are important for many reasons, it sets the scene. If your character is say... In a room. What are they doing? Are they looking out the window? Are they nursing a cup of coffee? Verbs paint the scene in your readers head, and let's their imagination do the work.

Adverbs!

The API of verbs and adjectives, it's the middle man, an adjective that describes the verb. If your character speaking, are they doing it happily? Angrily? Is their voice shaky? An adverb adds depth to an action, and flavor to just 'this character does this.' It's very important as it also gives you a look into the character. A little magnifying glass on their personality. Whether they're nervous or if they're bad at lying.
