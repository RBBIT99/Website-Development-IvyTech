Fight scenes are an odd sort of thing. Nobody seems to get them right. From injuries suffered in battle to just choreography in general (unless you're writing a fantasy realm with soft magic, I do not want to hear your character triple backflipping off a normal dude just to perfectly nail his eyes with two daggers.)

Unfortunately I haven't given myself enough time to research it yet myself, however I have collected a valueable resource

FightWrite!

This specific website holds blogs, podcasts, and books for writing fight scenes, including realistic scenes on how a fight would play out, magic or no magic. They even offer classes! I highly recommend checking them out.
