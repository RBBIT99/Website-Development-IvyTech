Habit Writing!

This has been one of my more recent hyperfixations. Writing habits. Nervous ticks, from picking at a sleeve to always adverting their eyes when someone looks at them. This isn't exactly necessary to write, not unless you want it to be. If your character is popular enough, people are gonna 'headcanon' these habits. Sometimes they're right, sometimes they aren't.

If the habit is important to their character, if it's a defining trait, sure. Write it. Otherwise it's just filler. Some habits don't need to be immediately announced either. It could be a brief mention, and some readers may realize it, some may only realize it until another character notices it. For example, say a character bites her lip every single time she's nervous, and someone else notices it after a very anxiety inducing time (like finals). They may point it out, ask if the character's alright. You can of course sprinkle it in before hand. Maybe it's written that the character does it every time, but it wasn't made obvious. It depends on the writer.
