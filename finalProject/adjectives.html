Adjectives! They're the key to defining your character. Is your character slow? Fast? Are they small, or tall?

Are they strong? Are they weak? All these things are keys to defining the character, and the more you can define your character, the more you can get people immersed in the story. Properly defining your characters means your readers know more. More about what they look like. How they dress, how they look. It creates this image of your character in their head
