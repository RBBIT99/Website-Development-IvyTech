Hello! I'm Alexander, the one writing this small blog. I'm a fledgling coder and avid writer with one goal in mind when it comes to my writing. Make someone cry.

Sounds wrong, I'm aware. My goal in detail is to write a story so immersive and relatable to my viewers, that a moment in the story will bring them to tears. However, writing well is complicated! Emotions and proper immersion is hard to keep in stories, and with everyone telling you what to do and what not to do, it's hard to sort out the noise from what's a good idea and what's a bad one.

Thus, I'm writing this blog! This blog is a personal reliquary of everything I picked up, with sources and websites for you to, in the words of a wise man, 'not stop here!' I'm hoping this website works not only for me to keep track of my notes, but as a way for others to learn these little intricacies in a way I could never write.
